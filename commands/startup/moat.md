Run the Moat Assessment from `~/.claude/skills/startup-metrics/SKILL.md` (Stage 7: Moat).

1. Understand the product and market (read project context if available)
2. Score each of the 7 moat types (0-5) with specific evidence
3. Calculate total moat score and strength rating
4. Run the "Can a big company copy this?" test
5. Identify the weakest moat and recommend deepening strategies
6. Identify the strongest moat and recommend how to extend it

Be brutally honest. Most early-stage startups have no moat — that's ok, but they need to know it and be building toward one.
