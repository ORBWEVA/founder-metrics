---

Run the Growth Curve analysis from `~/.claude/skills/startup-metrics/SKILL.md` (Stage 6: Growth).

1. Ask for their key metric (MRR, users, or revenue) over the last 6-12 months
2. Calculate MoM growth rates and the trend (accelerating, steady, decelerating)
3. Identify the curve type (J-curve, hockey stick, linear, S-curve, flat/declining)
4. Identify which phase they're in within that curve
5. Grade their growth rate against benchmarks (A+ to F)
6. Explain what typically happens next in their curve type
7. Recommend actions appropriate to their current phase

Don't confuse "early J-curve dip" with "failing." But also don't let founders call a flat line "pre-hockey-stick."
