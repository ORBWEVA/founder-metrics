Run the Runway analysis from `~/.claude/skills/startup-metrics/SKILL.md` (Stage 4: Runway).

1. Ask for: cash in bank, monthly expenses, monthly revenue
2. Calculate net burn rate and runway in months
3. Answer the "default alive?" question (at current growth rate, will you hit profitability before cash runs out?)
4. Model three scenarios: maintain current, cut 20%, accelerate spending
5. Compare burn rate to stage benchmarks
6. Give a clear verdict using the decision tree (safe / plan ahead / urgent / emergency)

This is the most emotionally loaded metric. Be direct but not alarmist. Give them actionable options, not panic.
