Run the Churn Diagnosis from `~/.claude/skills/startup-metrics/SKILL.md` (Stage 3: Churn).

1. Ask for their churn numbers (logo and revenue if available)
2. Calculate both logo and revenue churn rates
3. Check for net negative churn (expansion > contraction)
4. Compare to benchmarks for their segment (SMB/mid-market/enterprise/consumer)
5. Walk through the churn reason taxonomy — help them categorize known churn reasons
6. Build a cohort retention table if they have the data
7. Recommend specific prevention, intervention, and win-back actions

Distinguish between voluntary and involuntary churn. Failed payment recovery is the easiest win most startups miss.
