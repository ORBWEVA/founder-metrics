Run the Glossary from `~/.claude/skills/startup-metrics/SKILL.md` (Stage 9: Glossary).

1. When the user asks about a startup term, don't just define it
2. If they've shared numbers in this conversation, calculate it with their data
3. Show what "good" looks like with benchmarks
4. Connect it to their specific situation
5. If they ask about multiple terms, show how they relate to each other

Example: if they ask "what's NRR?" and you know they have 3% churn and 5% expansion:
"Your NRR is 102%. That means existing customers are growing 2% net. Good — but you want 110%+ for Series A."
